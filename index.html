About

Our journey at a glimpse!

  • June 2016

    CRIBBL is founded

    Amongst the mundane monotony of daily mid-semesters and club work, the idea of a start-up haunted every now and then, whenever the mind was freed from the general issues. One night, scribbling on a piece of paper about long and short term goals, was born 'CRIBBL'. And being a quarter engineer & a 'tech-savy' Y-generation IT guy, what could have been better than providing web services!

  • July 2016

    We set our Aim
